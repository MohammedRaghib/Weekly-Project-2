MY WEEKEND

A Weekend in the Wild

The sun was just beginning its descent, casting long, dancing shadows across the dense jungle.
I stood at the edge of a small clearing, my heart pounding with a mix of anticipation and fear.
This was it. The culmination of months of planning, training, and unwavering determination.
I was about to embark on a solo expedition into the heart of the Amazon rainforest.

Into the unknown


My backpack, heavy but well-packed, felt secure on my shoulders.
I checked my supplies one last time: compass, machete, first aid kit, and most importantly, my journal.
I would need to document every detail of this journey, to capture the raw beauty and the inherent dangers of this
untamed wilderness..
